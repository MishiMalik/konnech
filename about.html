About Us

At Konnech, election logistic software is all we do. For nearly 20 years we have been refining our software solutions based on the feedback and advice from the election managers we partner with and can confidently say that our technology is among the most effective, nuanced, and cost-saving in the industry. From Konnech’s first-client partnership with the City of Detroit to the 32 clients we currently work with in North America, our Founder and President, Eugene Yu, has implemented a client-centered approach. We listen to what you need in a system and will configure and customize our products so that you receive the exact solution you need.

That’s The Konnech Difference

We are proud to say that we have a passionate and dedicated of election software experts that will bring your software solution to fruition on the timeline you request. We are the developers, we are the designers, we are the coders, and we are project management experts. No other company provides better election logistic software solutions than Konnech.
